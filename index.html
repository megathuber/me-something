The Thompson Hotel

We loved the Thompson Chicago! With views of Lake Michigan and located walking distance from Michigan Ave shopping and our favorite restaurants the Thompson Chicago hotel is hard to beat. The rooms are modern and comfortable with really nice bathrooms. They also have a fitness center and restaurant on site.

Outstanding Restaurants

We splurged on food! The restaurants are part of the experience in Chicago so we plan ahead to eat at our favorites. Hugo's Frog Bar and Dika's Restaurant were the main two on this trip and they did not disappoint. However, even the Original Pancake House was fun. It's this little place in the midst of all the tall city buildings. And I love the little tea house, Argo Tea Cafe, situated in the median of a busy street creating a cozy courtyard in the middle of all of the hustle and bustle. That's Chicago.
